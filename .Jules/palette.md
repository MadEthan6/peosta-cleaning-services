## 2024-05-14 - Accessibility for icon-only buttons
**Learning:** In JobChecklist, the trash and add buttons were icon-only without aria-labels. Adding aria-labels is crucial for screen readers to understand the button's action.
**Action:** Next time I see an icon-only button, I will ensure it has an `aria-label` and `title` to explain its action.
